how to use

1.create your Account
2.login to your account
3.upload your CSV
4.load your CSV (then it will display few rows in html table) do not worry the way it display your csv ! just is to give 
as idea how we will map csv field to the field we have in our datatabase table
5.map table by click any field you want to map from csv file to datatabase table field
6.Then click import
7.after that file finish to be imported to datatabase table system will show us error or invalid field
then we click continue to remove those invalid or error field
8.then here you will be able to sort/group ,export preview,save file too
