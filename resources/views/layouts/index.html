SBV VALUES TRIANGLES

* Invest Bitcoins between R5500- R1 000 000 as a creator and invite two participants with the same values. (Primary triangles)

* Once the three values stores are full, the creator receives 60% growth and a R1000 called value creation manager’s bonus. This bonus adds up to R5000 after creating 5 triangles it becomes available for withdrawal

* The triangle splits thus allowing participants 1 and 2 to form two separate secondary triangles and at this point they become creators.

* Once they bring their two participants each, they receive their 60% growth and R1000 value creation manager’s bonus.

* Participants can either withdraw, create new triangles or upgrade to super triangles.

SBV SUPER TRIANGLES

* Invest Bitcoins between R 9 300- R1 000 000 as a creator and invite two participants with the same values. (Primary triangles)

* Once the three values stores are full, the creator receives 100% growth and R2000 value creation manager’s bonus.

* The triangle splits thus allowing participants 1 and 2 to form two separate secondary triangle and at this point they become creators.

* Once they invite two participants each, they receive their 100% growth.

* Participants can either withdraw, create new triangles or upgrade to super triangles.

SBV GUIDE

1.Make sure that you provide a working email address during your registration

2.Activate your account by paying R200 registration fee.

3.Select your investment and pay between R5500-R1 000 000

4.Add standard user number 1 and 2, make sure their email addresses are correct so that they can receive their login details.

5.Once the triangle is complete, it splits and the creator is paid instantly

6.Participants may use their earnings either to reinvest, create new triangles or upgrade to super triangles.

SCHEMATIC FLOW

  • PRIMARY VALUE TRIANGLE

    Register and start a triangle by inviting two participants. Add the two participants in order to complete the triangle. All three value stores should be funded so that the triangle could split. Once the triangle splits, the creator get paid instantly depending on amount invested.

  • SECONDARY VALUE TRIANGLE

    User 1 comes from primary value triangle and creates and leads a secondary value triangle. User 1 invites two participants and the triangle splits. User 1 gets paid instantly depending on amount invested.

  • SECONDARY VALUE TRIANGLE

    User 2 comes from primary value triangle as well and creates and leads a secondary value triangle. User 2 invites two participants and the triangle splits. User 2 gets paid instantly on amounts invested.

  • Repetitive Cycle

    Triangles evolve through a repetitive cycle where every participant eventually becomes a creator. Super triangles and value triangles alike go through the same cycle.
